## Color Schemes

### Primary Colors
- Hex: #FF5733 (Orange)
- Hex: #C70039 (Red)

### Secondary Colors
- Hex: #900C3F (Dark Red)
- Hex: #581845 (Purple)

### Accent Colors
- Hex: #FFC300 (Yellow)
- Hex: #DAF7A6 (Light Green)

### Branding Guidelines
- Use primary colors for main elements like headers, footers, and buttons.
- Use secondary colors for backgrounds and secondary elements.
- Use accent colors for highlights, links, and interactive elements.

## Frontend Pages

### 1. Home Page
**Layout**: Header, main banner, featured drawings, categories, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Main banner image, featured drawings gallery, category thumbnails.

### 2. Shop Page
**Layout**: Sidebar filters, grid/list view of drawings, pagination, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Drawings thumbnails, filters for categories, price, and ratings.

### 3. Product Detail Page
**Layout**: Image gallery, product details, price, add to cart button, related products, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Breadcrumbs, menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: High-resolution images, product description, price, reviews, related products.

### 4. Cart Page
**Layout**: List of selected products, totals, checkout button, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Product thumbnails, names, prices, quantities, subtotal, total, checkout button.

### 5. Checkout Page
**Layout**: Billing details form, shipping details form, payment method selection, order summary, place order button, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Form fields for billing and shipping details, payment methods, order summary.

### 6. About Us Page
**Layout**: Team photos, company history, mission statement, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Text about the company, team member photos and bios, mission statement.

### 7. Contact Page
**Layout**: Contact form, contact details, map, header, footer.
**Navigation**: Menu with links to Home, Shop, About Us, Contact, and Cart.
**Content**: Form fields for name, email, subject, message, company contact details, embedded map.

## Content Requirements

### Text
- Product descriptions
- Company history and mission
- Team member bios

### Images
- High-resolution product images
- Team photos
- Banners and promotional graphics

### Videos
- Product demonstration videos
- Company introduction video
